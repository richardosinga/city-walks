One of the most beautiful of Amsterdam's smaller canals, running west from the Prinsengracht into the Jordaan. The Flower Canal — bloemgracht means flower canal — has well-preserved 17th-century gabled houses and a row of houseboats. It is quieter and more local in character than the main tourist canals, a good place to understand what Amsterdam looks like when it isn't performing for visitors.
