A single linden tree on Eerste Leliedwarsstraat, planted to mark the liberation of Amsterdam on 5 May 1945. In a neighbourhood where memory sits close to the surface — the Anne Frank House is ten minutes' walk away, the Homomonument is around the corner — this tree is the quietest memorial of all. It is easy to walk past without noticing. Worth stopping for.
