The former Heineken brewery on Stadhouderskade 78, founded in 1867 and in continuous production until 1988, when the company moved to a larger facility on the outskirts of the city. The red-brick building still carries the Heineken name on its facade. Since 1991 it has operated as a visitor centre and museum tracing the history of the brewery and the brewing process. It is one of the most-visited attractions in Amsterdam.
