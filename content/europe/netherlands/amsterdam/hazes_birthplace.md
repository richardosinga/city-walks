The birthplace of André Hazes, born here on 30 June 1951 to a violent, barely-employed father in one of De Pijp's cheapest tenement blocks. Aged eight, he began singing on the Albert Cuypmarkt for coins to buy his mother a birthday present. On Liberation Day 1959, comedian Johnny Kraaykamp Sr. heard him there and brought him on national television. When Hazes died in 2004, 48,000 people attended his public funeral at Amsterdam Arena, watched live by 8 million viewers.
