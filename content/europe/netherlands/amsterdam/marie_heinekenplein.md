A small square directly south of the former Heineken brewery, lined with terrace cafes that fill up in good weather. Named for Marie Heineken, wife of brewery founder Gerard Adriaan Heineken. The square marks the northern edge of De Pijp proper, where the wide boulevard of Stadhouderskade gives way to the narrower streets and four-storey brick buildings of the neighbourhood.
